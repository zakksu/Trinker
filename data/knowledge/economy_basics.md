# Economy & Villager Management

AoE2 is won on eco efficiency long before fights decide games.

## Golden rules
- **Villager count** is the strongest predictor of mid-game power on standard maps.
- On wood/gold: place camps touching resources; walk time is hidden villager idle time.
- Floating resources above 200 food/wood usually means you should spend on units or upgrades.

## Feudal transition
- Queue farms before clicking Feudal if your build relies on steady food income.
- Build a blacksmith or barracks only when your build order says so — not when you feel pressured.

## When behind
- Add one defensive building rather than panic-clicking Feudal or Castle.
- Match opponent scout count before adding extra military if you are still on plan.
