# Scout Rush & Early Pressure

Scout rushes punish greedy eco and sloppy walling.

## Execution checklist
- Feudal on plan (typically 8:00–9:30 for 18–20 pop scouts).
- First two scouts target enemy eco — avoid tower diving without numbers.
- Keep production — idle stables lose the timing window.

## Defense reminder
- Wall critical gaps before Feudal if scout is spotted.
- Spearmen counter scouts; one range + spears holds most early scout floods.

## TRINKER tip
If your feudal is late, skip full scout flood and add fewer scouts + armor upgrade instead.
