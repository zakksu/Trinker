# Feudal Age Timing

Most meta openers aim for Feudal between **7:30 and 10:00** game time depending on build.

## Dark Age fundamentals
- Never idle your Town Center — queue villagers continuously until Feudal click.
- Place houses before you need pop cap; a blocked TC is lost seconds you cannot recover.
- Lure boar early on open maps; delay boar if you lose two villagers to the boar fight.

## Common mistakes
- Clicking Feudal with fewer than 500 food banked for a standard 22-pop build.
- Forgetting to reassign hunters after boar is depleted.
- Building military before economy is stable (unless doing a drush).

## Drill
Pick one build and hit the same feudal window three games in a row before switching civs.
