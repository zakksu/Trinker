# Fast Castle — TRINKER knowledge snippet

## Goal
Skip early military, boom economy, reach Castle Age with map control.

## Dark → Feudal
- 27+2 or similar: prioritize steady TC production.
- Wall or tower if drushed; do not over-react with full military.
- Feudal around **10:00–10:30** is acceptable for FC builds.

## Feudal → Castle
- Two ranges OR market + blacksmith for upgrades path.
- Add 2–4 farms; second lumber camp when wood dips.
- Castle click when you have 800+ food banked and stable eco.

## Castle Age
- Drop first TC within 60s of Castle if safe.
- Knight or unique unit production depending on civ.
- Avoid floating 200+ food while aging — queue vills or farms.

## Common mistakes
- Too many vils on gold before Castle (starving food).
- No map vision — lose Castle timing to a timing attack.
- Adding military in Feudal when the plan is pure FC.
