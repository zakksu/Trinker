# Archer Rush — TRINKER knowledge snippet

## Goal
Reach Feudal Age quickly, then produce archers from two ranges for early pressure.

## Dark Age
- Standard 22-pop build: 6 on sheep, then wood, then boars.
- First house at 14 pop; second before Feudal click.
- Research Loom before second boar if low HP villagers.

## Feudal timing
- Target Feudal click around **9:00–9:30** on Arabia.
- Queue two vills to gold before clicking up if going double range.
- Build blacksmith + two ranges; Fletching before first fight if possible.

## Common mistakes
- Forgetting farm transition after first lumber camp.
- Adding houses too late and blocking archer production.
- Fighting before Fletching when opponent has scouts.
